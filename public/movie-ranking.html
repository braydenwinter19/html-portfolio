The Best Movies According to Brayden Winter

My top 3 favorite movies off of the top of my head

Jurassic Park

As a child I loved dinosaurs, and this movie was like an inspiration to me. It still kind of is, honestly. Pure genius screenwriting, i wish modern movies were like this. The book was also great.

School of Rock

Jack Black is just a great actor altogether. Another childhood favorite.

Harry Potter - All of them as a whole

Harry Potter is really magical, creating a whole world of fantasy in the watcher's mind.
